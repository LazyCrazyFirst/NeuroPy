Some Story

The first works in which the main results on the creation of neural networks were obtained were done by McCallock and Pitts. In 1943, they developed a computer model of a neural network based on mathematical algorithms and the theory of brain activity.

They hypothesized that neurons could be simplistically viewed as devices that operate with binary numbers, and called this model “threshold logic”. In 1949, Donald Hebb created the first neural network algorithm that has been used for more than 30 years before Frank Rosenblatt invented the perceptron.

A perceptron is a system that mimics all the processes inside the brain. It is the perceptron mechanism that is fundamental in creating neural networks. ANNs are finding more and more applications. Using trained neural networks, a large number of software products have been developed. the promise of artificial intelligence creates a huge number of jobs for qualified professionals.
